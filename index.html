AngularJS based app used for filtering and viewing trend reports

About the project

The research page of richlinecreative.com is a password protected AngularJS application that allows for the searching and filtering of a collection of fashion trend PDF's. The banner section of the page is a powered by a PHP CMS to simply aid in the updating to the banner images that link to featured articles.

A single controller is used to hold the collection and upon selecting a filter items are copied into a temporary array used by the view.
